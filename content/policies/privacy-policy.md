# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** May 25, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Lloyd & Vale handles information
collected through www.lloydvale.com and through direct correspondence
at support@lloydvale.com.

Lloyd & Vale operates as a business-to-business resource for shop
equipment, consumables, technical support, and related operator
content. The website is not a consumer storefront.

## Information We Collect

We collect information that is reasonably related to responding to
inquiries, operating the website, and maintaining ordinary business
records.

- **Information you provide.** When you submit a quote request, a
  support request, or email us directly, we receive the contact and
  business details you choose to provide, along with the contents of
  your message. This typically includes name, email address, company
  or business information if provided, and the details of your
  request.
- **Information collected automatically.** When you visit the
  website, our hosting and email-delivery providers may process
  ordinary technical information such as IP address, browser type,
  request timestamps, and routing information. This information is
  used to operate, secure, and troubleshoot the website and the
  forms.

We do not collect payment card information through this website.

## How We Use Information

We use the information described above to:

- respond to quote requests, support requests, and direct emails;
- communicate with you about your inquiry;
- operate, maintain, and secure the website and its forms;
- keep records of correspondence in the ordinary course of business.

We do not sell personal information. We do not use the information
for behavioral advertising.

## How We Share Information

We share information only as needed to operate the website and
respond to inquiries:

- **Service providers.** We use third-party providers for website
  hosting and email delivery. These providers process information on
  our behalf and only as needed to deliver their services.
- **Legal and safety reasons.** We may disclose information when
  required by law, to respond to lawful requests, or to protect the
  rights, property, or safety of Lloyd & Vale or others.
- **Business transfers.** If Lloyd & Vale is involved in a merger,
  acquisition, or sale of assets, information may be transferred as
  part of that transaction.

## Cookies and Similar Technologies

Lloyd & Vale does not intentionally use advertising or analytics
cookies at this stage of the website. Hosting and security providers
may process limited technical information that is necessary to
deliver the website and protect it from abuse. If this changes, we
will update this section.

## Data Retention

We retain inquiry correspondence and related records for as long as
reasonably necessary to respond to the inquiry, operate our
business, and meet our record-keeping needs. Technical server
information is retained for shorter operational periods as
determined by our hosting and email providers.

## Your Choices

You may contact support@lloydvale.com at any time with questions
about your information or to request that we update or remove
correspondence we hold about you, subject to our reasonable
record-keeping needs.

California residents or other users with privacy questions may
contact us at support@lloydvale.com. We will respond to reasonable
requests in a reasonable timeframe.

## International Users

Lloyd & Vale operates from the United States and is intended for
United States business users. If you contact us from outside the
United States, you understand that your information will be
processed in the United States.

## Children

The website is intended for business users and is not directed to
children. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we
will revise the "Last Updated" date at the top of the page.

## Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy may be sent to
support@lloydvale.com.
