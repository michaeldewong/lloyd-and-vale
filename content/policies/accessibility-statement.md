# Accessibility Statement

**Last Updated:** May 25, 2026

Lloyd & Vale aims to make its website usable and accessible to a
broad range of visitors, including those who rely on assistive
technologies.

## Our Approach

The Lloyd & Vale website is guided by the principles of the Web
Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 at Level AA where
practical. We treat accessibility as part of ordinary site
maintenance rather than a one-time project.

This statement reflects a commitment, not a claim of full
conformance. We have not completed a formal third-party audit at
this stage.

## Known Limitations

As Lloyd & Vale continues to develop the website, some pages,
images, or interactive elements may not yet meet every WCAG 2.1 AA
criterion. We work to improve these areas over time.

## Feedback and Assistance

If you have difficulty accessing any part of the website, or if you
need information from the website in a different format, please
contact us:

support@lloydvale.com

We will make reasonable efforts to provide the requested information
or to improve access. Please include the page or content involved and
a brief description of the issue so we can respond accurately.

## Contact

General accessibility questions may be sent to
support@lloydvale.com.
